About me

Hi, my name is Lixing Lyu. I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. degree in Institute of Operations Research and Analytics (IORA), National University of Singapore (NUS). I am fortunate to be advised by Wang Chi Cheung and Chung Piaw Teo. My research focuses on online learning, optimization, and their applications in operations research. Prior to joining NUS, I obtained my B.S. degree from the School of the Gifted Young (SGY), University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), majoring in apply mathematics and minoring in computer science. I also have industry experiences. From Oct. 2020 to Apr. 2021, I interned in Algorithms Innovation Lab, Cloud BU, Huawei as an algorithm engineer.
