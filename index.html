Biography

Lixing Lyu was born in Guangzhou, one of the largest and most international cities in China. He is currently pursuing his Ph.D. degree in the Institute of Operations Research and Analytics (IORA), National University of Singapore (NUS). His research interests include Optimization, Operations Research, Machine Learning, and their related areas and applications. Prior to joining NUS, he obtained his B.S. degree from the School of the Gifted Young (SGY), University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), majoring in Apply Mathematics and minoring in Computer Science. He also has industry experiences. From Oct. 2020 to Apr. 2021, he interned in Algorithms Innovation Lab, Cloud BU, Huawei as an algorithm engineer.
