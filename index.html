Biography

Lixing Lyu was born in Guangzhou, one of the largest and most international cities in China. He is currently pursuing his B.S. degree from the School of the Gifted Young, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). He is majored in Applied Mathematics and minored in Computer Science. Prior to joining USTC, he graduated from Middle School affiliated to Guangzhou University. He also has industry experiences. He did his internship in Algorithms Innovation Lab, Cloud BU, Huawei from 2020.10 to 2021.04.

Research Interest

Lixing Lyu's research interest includes Optimization, Operations Research, Machine Learning, and their related areas. More specifically, he is interested in discrete optimization and machine learning, and their applications in scheduling problems, queueing theory and network design.

News

Lixing Lyu has accepted the offer from Institute of Operations Research and Analytics (IORA) in National University of Singapore (NUS), and he will be a Ph.D. candidate since 2021.08.
